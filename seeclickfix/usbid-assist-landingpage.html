Report a Problem - Union Square BID Assist

Overview

Now you can report non-emergency quality-of-life issues in Union Square from your smartphone, tablet, or computer, thanks to Union Square BID Assist. This new program allow you to report issues related to:

  • Graffiti
  • Illegal Dumping
  • Safety Concern
  • Noise Complaint
  • Abandoned Vehicle
  • Aggressive Panhandling
  • Bicycle Code Violation
  • Tresspassing / Loitering
  • Vandalism
  • Street Light Out
  • Trash
  • ...
  • ...
  • ...
  • ...
  • ...
  • ...

With each service request, we encourage you to attach a photo or video, as a visual reference. Union Square BID staff responds to requests Monday through Friday between 9am and 6pm. If this report comes in on a holiday, weekend or after-hours, it will be read on the next business day. NOTE: for emergencies or otherurgent matters that require immediate attention or a police response, dial 9-1-1.

Desktop

The widget above allows you to make service requests, and well as discover existing problems that have been reported, from your desktop computer. To report an issue:
  1. Make sure the Report tab is selected .
  2. Drag the map marker to the location of your issue, or type in the address directly.
  3. Click Next.
  4. Choose a service request Category from the drop-down menu that matches your issue.
  5. Enter a Title (required), additional Description (optional), and attach an Image (optional).
  6. Click Submit.
