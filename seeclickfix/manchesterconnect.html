Report a Problem - Manchester Connect

Overview

Now you can help report non-emergency problems to the City of Manchester from your smartphone, tablet, or computer, thanks to our new partnership with SeeClickFix. Use the Manchester Connect program to submit service requests and ask questions related to:

  • Graffiti
  • Illegal Dumping
  • Pothole
  • Street Light Out
  • Traffic Signal/Sign Problem
  • Trash/Recycling
  • ...
  • ...
