Report a Problem - Greensboro Fix It

Overview

Help report non-emergency problems to the City of Greensboro from your smartphone, tablet, or desktop computer! Using Greensboro Fix It, you can make service requests such as:

  • potholes
  • graffiti
  • damaged street signs
  • abandoned vehicles
  • streetlight outages
  • traffic signals
  • parking violations
  • sidewalk issues
  • snow and ice removal
  • park issues

You have the option to attach a photo or video with each service request, to help better communicate the problem.

Our staff responds to these requests during regular business hours. If this report comes in on a holiday, weekend or after-hours, it will be read on the next business day. PLEASE NOTE: Greensboro Fix It is not to be used for emergencies. For urgent matters that require immediate attention or a police response, call 9-1-1.

Desktop

The interactive widget above allows you to make service requests, and well as discover existing problems that have been reported, from your desktop computer. To report an issue:
  1. Select the Report tab.
  2. Drag the map marker to the location of your issue, or type in the address directly.
  3. Click Next.
  4. Choose a service request Category from the drop-down menu that matches your issue.
  5. Enter a Title (required), additional Description (optional), and attach an Image (optional).
  6. Click Submit.
