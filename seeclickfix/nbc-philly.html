Overview

Is there a street light out in your neighborhood? Abandoned vehicle? Graffiti that needs removal? A pothole that needs paving? Report non-emergency problems from your smartphone or computer with SeeClickFix.

Report an Issue

Instructions

 The widget below allows you to make service requests, and well as discover existing problems that have been reported, from your desktop computer. To report an issue:
  1. Click the green Report an Issue button.
  2. Drag the map marker to the location of your issue, or type in the address directly.
  3. Click Next.
  4. Enter a Title (required), additional Description (optional), and attach an Image (optional).
  5. Click Submit.

Support

For technical support with the SeeClickFix platform, please email support@seeclickfix.com.

Once you have sent a report, you will receive email communication from the city acknowledging your service request. You can continue to check on the status of the issue online. Once the work is complete or the issue has been resolved, you will be notified by email as well.
